Welcome to Educative!

Are you an experienced React developer, or perhaps a seasoned Product Manager? Do you have a passion for sharing your knowledge and experience to give others a headstart in their careers? However, you are intimidated by the plethora of online E-learning platforms that are out there. If you feel that the learners could get more value out of a more hands-on approach towards content, then you're in the right place.

How is that so you ask? Let us take you on a journey across the Eduverse where we explore all the amazing stuff you can do on our platform. Here is a glimpse of what Educative has to offer:

Test your learners

No learning experience is complete without fun and interactive exercise. Solving these challenges would give users a sense of accomplishment. Our platform allows you to create such challenges in any language of your choice, ensuring that learners get the most out of your course.

Build full-stack web applications on-the-fly

Web development is one of the most sought-after skills in the modern CS landscape. With our Single Page Application widget, web development has never been easier. Enable your learners to work with any web framework directly in the browser. No prior setup required!
